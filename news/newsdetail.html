CoreNet Global, the most prominent professional association for corporate real estate executives, today announced the winner of its H. Gordon Wyllie, MCR, Young Leader of the Year Award. The award was presented during the association’s North American Global Summit being held this week in Seattle, Wash.

The award, named for one of the founders of a CoreNet Global predecessor organization, was presented to Rosario Carbonell, MCR, Executive Director, KMC Savills, Inc. Carbonell mentors and trains the tenant representation services team within KMC Savills.  She also helped develop the business model for KMC’s sister company, KMC Solutions, giving KMC MAG Group a competitive edge in the service market.

As a pioneer in the company, Carbonell has helped it grow, advanced many of its strategies and assisted in generating its client base, contributing significantly to the company’s 45 percent revenue growth from 2014. Under her management, closed sales totaled 149,838 square meters (1.6 million square feet) since 2014. Her strong leadership skills have cultivated a staff of more than 100 with a low attrition rate and high employee engagement.

Carbonell was nominated for the award by the CoreNet Global Philippines Chapter.

Also at its Global Awards and Recognition Dinner, CoreNet Global honored two finalists for the award: , Associate Vice President, HGA Architects and Engineers, CoreNet Global Northern California Chapter; and , Marketing and Communication China Manager, CBRE, CoreNet Global China Chapter.
