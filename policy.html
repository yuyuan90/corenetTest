All CoreNet Global membership lists are a members-only benefit, and their use is limited to professional networking and individual networking only - not for selling or mass solicitation. Any misuse of these lists including their reproduction either electronically or manually constitutes a copyright infringement against CoreNet Global and its members.

The information you provide will be used to keep you informed about events, news, and relevant information regarding CoreNet Global and will not be sold or disclosed to any third party.

E-mail Communication

As a member of CoreNet Global, you understand that CoreNet Global will send you e-mails periodically, and these electronic communications may contain messages or links to third party websites. If you choose to visit a third-party website, you do so of your own free will. CoreNet Global does not govern information collected on third-party websites, and we assume no responsibility for any misuse of your information by any third party.

CoreNet Global is in accordance with the CAN-SPAM Act of 2003 and intends to be a leader in ensuring members' privacy and will unsubscribe members from our database in a timely fashion.
