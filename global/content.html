全球企业不动产协会

 

我们是谁？

CoreNet Global is a non-profit association, headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia (US), representing more than 10,000 executives in 50 countries with strategic responsibility for the real estate assets of large corporations. The organization’s mission is to advance the practice of corporate real estate through professional development opportunities, publications, research, conferences, designations and networking in 47 local chapters and networking groups globally.

什么是企业不动产？

Corporate real estate is the real property used by a company for its own operational purposes. It provides corporations with a productive environment to house employees, manufacture and distribute products and provide services to the market. Corporate real estate touches all classes of property, land and buildings such as office facilities, data centers, manufacturing facilities, logistic centers, corporate headquarters, distribution facilities, retail stores and hotels. 



我们的使命

  • CoreNet Global's mission is to advance the practice of corporate real estate through professional development opportunities, publications, research, conferences, and chapters and networking groups
  • CoreNet Global aims to broaden its network, raise the profile of corporate real estate, serve as the profession's voice and thought leader and champion the profession's development and maturity
